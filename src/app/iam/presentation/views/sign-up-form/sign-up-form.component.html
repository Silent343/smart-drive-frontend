SmartDrive Finance Privacy Policy

SmartDrive Finance processes personal and company data to create company accounts, authenticate administrators and sellers, simulate vehicle credits, generate reports, and keep an audit trail of confirmed sales.

The platform may process administrator and seller names, DNI, phone, company RUC, company domain, generated usernames, credit simulation data, vehicle data and client identifiers. Passwords are never stored in plain text.

DNI and public identifiers are protected at rest by the backend using reversible AES encryption so the authorized UI can show them when needed. Access is role-based: administrators manage company defaults, sellers inherit those values and cannot edit them, and each company only sees its own sellers and confirmed reports.

Data is retained while the company account remains active or while required for operational, legal or audit purposes. Authorized users may request correction, access or removal through their company administrator.
